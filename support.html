The Founding HERD

For over ten years, we discussed the possibility of launching a women-centered storytelling platform. In 2023, we decided it was time to make it happen. We launched HERD, and the response was energizing and affirming. In fact, our first event completely sold out in just three days!

Bringing the HERD concept to life was incredibly rewarding, but also expensive. From branding to web hosting to equipment, the costs added up. We were grounded in the fact that when we work collectively and pool our resources, we lighten the load. We asked our community to become HERD Founding Members.

Our Founding Members' one-time contributions helped offset start-up costs and ensure that HERD thrives. They received some very cool perks, but even more so, they have our everlasting gratitude for their belief in us and for believing in the power of bringing women together. It's nothing short of magic when strong, fabulous women convene and celebrate one another!
