HERD is an innovative storytelling platform that empowers women to share their stories with an enthusiastic and supportive audience. Through the art of storytelling and active listening, HERD fosters a sense of community, providing women with a space to connect, find common ground, and discover their voice.

ATTEND

HERD hosts regular events featuring amateur storytelling performances by women, for women. Join us to listen, share, laugh, weep, bond, and connect!

SHARE

You can simply be part of the audience and support the storytellers or throw your name into the hat to be one of the featured "Yaks."

REVEL

HERD provides a safe and empowering space for women to share their stories— there will be no judging or winners. We draw inspiration from other storyslams like NPR's The Moth, but with a women-centered, joy-forward focus.
