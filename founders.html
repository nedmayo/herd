Thank you Herd Founders!

For more than a decade, we considered creating a women-centered storytelling platform. This year, we turned that idea into reality with the launch of HERD, and the enthusiastic response has been incredible. Our initial event sold out completely within just three days!

We are so grateful for your faith in our vision and in the power of women connecting. The energy created when strong, amazing women come together to support each other is truly magical. We deeply appreciate the following HERD friends, badasses, bosses, sheros, and matriarchs who have championed us and are essential to HERD's continued growth and success.
