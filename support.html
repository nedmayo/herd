Join the Founding HERD

For over ten years, we have discussed the possibility of launching a women-centered storytelling platform. This year, we decided it was time to make it happen. We launched HERD, and the response has been energizing and affirming. In fact, our first event completely sold out in just three days!

Bringing the HERD concept to life has been incredibly rewarding, but also expensive. From branding to web hosting to equipment, the costs add up. We are grounded in the fact that when we work collectively and pool our resources, we lighten the load. Therefore, we are humbly asking you to become a HERD Founding Member.

As a HERD Founding Member, your one-time contribution will help offset start-up costs and ensure that HERD thrives. You will receive some very cool perks*, but even more so, you will have our everlasting gratitude for your belief in us and for believing in the power of bringing women together. It's nothing short of magic when strong, fabulous women convene and celebrate one another!

SO PLEASE CONSIDER BECOMING A HERD FOUNDING MEMBER!
